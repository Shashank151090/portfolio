MDM is mobile device management application which takes care of devices alert related to FOTA and diagnostics. It manages all the users who are using the devices according to permissions of those users. Users can manage alerts and polling of those alerts to device with the help of this application. Polling, provisioning and license updates are performed using MDM. MDM is designed to view the data of devices in charts and diagrammatic representation as well.
